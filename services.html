MCC Panels - Motor Control Center

We provide very effective MCC Panels for smooth control of electric motors in central location.

‍Salient Features:
- A motor control center (MCC) is an assembly to control some or all electric motors in a central location. It consists of multiple enclosed sections having a common power bus and with each section containing a combination starter, which in turn consists of motor starter, fuses or circuit breaker, and power disconnect.
- A motor control center can also include push buttons, indicator lights, variable-frequency drives, programmable logic controllers, and metering equipment.
-It may be combined with the electrical service entrance for the building. MCC’s are typically found in large commercial or industrial buildings where there are many electric motors that need to be controlled from a central location, such as a mechanical room or electrical room.

Power Distribution Boards

Get the latest and effective Power Distribution Boards at affordable price.

‍Salient Features:
‍-A distribution board (also known as panelboard, breaker panel, or electric panel) is a component of an electricity supply system that divides an electrical power feed into subsidiary circuits, while providing a protective fuse or circuit breaker for each circuit in a common enclosure.
-Normally, a main switch, and in recent boards, one or more residual-current devices (RCD) or residual current breakers with overcurrent protection (RCBO), are also incorporated.

RTPFC Panels -  Thyristor  Swiched Real TIme Power Factor Correction panels

We provide RTPFC Panels that will prevent generation of switching spikes and other transient effects.

‍Salient Features:
‍- Real Time Power Factor Correction (RTPFC) Panels helps to compensate the reactive power for extremely rapid acquisition of the power factor within the range of 40-60 msec.-To avoid transient effects like waveform distortions, generation of switching spikes etc, the panels are designed to have smooth connections at zero crossing.
-Transient free capacitor group switching using Electronics Switching elements is the advantage of this product.
- It also helps in reduction of voltage flickering. Reactors are present in basic system which limit the inrush current.
- To protect the capacitor banks, iron core reactors are present that detune the network to prevent resonance. MCB/MCCB/HRC fuse protection installed at each step.

- APFC is an automatic power factor control panel which is used to improve the power factor. of capacitor Banks.
- APFC Panels consist of Fast Real Time Transient-Free switching of capacitor banks.-Saves Energy with P.F Control and Harmonic Filtration
